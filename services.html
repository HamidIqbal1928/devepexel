Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope. A simple WordPress site takes 2-3 weeks, while custom web applications can take 6-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines in our proposals.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes! All our packages include post-launch support. We offer monthly maintenance plans starting at $199/month covering updates, security, backups, and technical support.

What if I need changes after launch?

We offer 2 rounds of revisions included in all packages. Additional changes can be handled through our hourly rate ($99/hr) or monthly retainer agreements.

Do you work with clients outside your location?

Absolutely! We work with clients globally. All communication happens via video calls, email, and project management tools for seamless collaboration.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency. Projects are typically split: 50% upfront, 50% upon completion.

Can you help with content creation?

Yes! We have professional copywriters and content strategists on our team who can create SEO-optimized content, blog posts, and marketing materials.
